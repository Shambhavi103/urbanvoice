About UrbanVoice

Empowering citizens to build better cities through transparent and efficient public redressal.

Our Vision

We believe in cities where every citizen's voice is heard and every systemic issue is addressed efficiently. UrbanVoice bridges the gap between the public and the government by offering a streamlined, transparent, and direct platform for filing and tracking public grievances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the public redressal process. We provide real-time updates and seamless integration with related ministries, ensuring that your complaints are assigned to the right authorities promptly.

  • Instant Registration: Fast and reliable complaint logging with OTP validation.
  • Direct Linkage: Automatically routed to district-level authorities for swift action.
  • Full Transparency: A secure workflow that keeps you informed with real-time status updates.

Our Core Values

Transparency

Every step of the complaint resolution process is visible and accountable.

Efficiency

Our platform ensures that issues reach the right authorities instantly without paperwork.

Community

Empowering local communities to take charge and improve urban living together.
