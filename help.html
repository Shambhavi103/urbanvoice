How UrbanVoice Works

From filing a grievance to getting it resolved, learn everything about using our transparent platform.

The Process

We've designed UrbanVoice to be as straightforward as possible. Follow these simple steps to bring civic issues to light and track their resolution progress in real-time.

1. Secure Login & Verification

Begin by providing basic details such as your Full Name, Email, Phone Number, and Aadhar details. Enter the OTP to securely authenticate your identity and access your dashboard. Your privacy and verification are our priority.

2. File Your Complaint

Once logged in, clearly describe your issue. Optionally, provide the location address and upload supporting media such as images or videos (up to 10MB for images and 50MB for videos). Submitting comprehensive evidence speeds up the resolution.

3. Receive Registration ID

After submission, our system instantly assigns your grievance to the relevant authorities and provides you with a unique Registration ID. You'll need this ID to follow up on your complaint later. This Registration ID is also emailed to you for safekeeping.

4. Track Status Anytime

Use our real-time tracking feature on your dashboard. Simply enter your Registration ID to instantly see the current status (e.g., Pending, In Progress, Resolved) and any remarks provided by the officers reviewing your case.

5. Resolution & Feedback

Once your grievance reaches the "Resolved" status, the relevant local authority has officially addressed the situation. We continuously monitor officer responsiveness to keep city improvement transparent and accountable.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens if I lose my Registration ID?
    Don't worry, the unique Registration ID is immediately sent to the email address you provided during the login securely. Check your inbox to retrieve it.
  • Who looks at my complaints?
    Based on your selected State and District, your complaint is routed to the corresponding department (e.g., Municipality, Water Board) equipped to handle the specific category.
  • Why is Aadhar necessary for login?
    Aadhar verification minimizes spam, ensuring that government resources are efficiently allocated to legitimate issues raised by verified citizens.
